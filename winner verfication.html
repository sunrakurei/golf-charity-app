Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding winner verification and payouts.

How long does winner verification take?

Verification is usually completed within 24 to 72 hours after all required documents are submitted.

What documents are required?

Government ID, proof of address and bank details are required for successful verification.

Can a verification request be rejected?

Yes. Incorrect information or invalid documents may lead to rejection.

When is prize money released?

Prize payouts are processed after successful verification approval.
