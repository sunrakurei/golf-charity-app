Golf Score Tracking System

The platform allows members to submit and manage their most recent golf scores. To maintain accuracy and relevance, only the latest five scores are stored in the system.

  • Store only the latest 5 scores
  • Score range between 1 and 45
  • Date tracking included
  • Newest score displayed first
  • Oldest score automatically replaced

Monthly Draw & Reward System

Every month a transparent draw is conducted. Participants can win prizes based on matching draw numbers.

5 Number Match

Highest winning category receiving 40% of the total prize pool.

4 Number Match

Winners receive 35% of the available prize pool.

3 Number Match

Winners receive 25% of the available prize pool.

Charity Contribution Program

Every membership helps support registered charitable organizations. Members can select their preferred charity and increase their contribution percentage whenever desired.

  • Minimum 10% contribution
  • Flexible contribution options
  • Verified charity partners
  • Independent donations supported

Winner Verification Process

Upload Proof

Winners upload required proof or screenshots through their dashboard.

Admin Review

Submitted evidence is reviewed by platform administrators.

Prize Approval

Approved winners receive their rewards and payout status is updated.
